Our Story

Building India's Tender Intelligence Layer

We started TendRAI because we watched brilliant contractors lose crore-value tenders to paperwork errors and missed deadlines. We knew AI could fix that.

The Problem We're Solving

India's government procurement market is worth over ₹40 lakh crore annually. Yet most qualified contractors — especially MSMEs — lose out not because they can't do the work, but because they can't navigate the complex documentation requirements.

A single missing certificate, a wrong form, or a missed deadline can disqualify a perfectly capable contractor. TendRAI changes this with AI that reads tender documents so you don't have to.
