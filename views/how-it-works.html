How TendRAI Works

From registration to winning — get started in under 10 minutes and manage all your tenders with AI assistance.

1
Step One
Create Your Account
Register in under 2 minutes. Set up your company profile with registration details, certificates, and past experience. This data powers our AI personalization — the more you add, the smarter your recommendations.
2
Step Two
Discover & Import Tenders
Browse government portals or upload tender documents directly to TendRAI. Supports GeM, CPPP, NSWS, eProcurement systems, and 50+ state portals. Simply upload the PDF or paste the tender URL.
3
Step Three
Run AI Analysis
Our AI reads the entire tender document in seconds. It extracts requirements, identifies eligibility criteria, checks your compliance status, and generates a complete bid preparation checklist. You get a score showing your readiness to bid.
4
Step Four
Prepare Your Bid
Follow the AI-generated checklist to prepare documents. Upload files directly to TendRAI's secure storage. The compliance checker validates each document in real-time before you finalize your bid package.
5
Step Five
Track & Submit
Get deadline reminders, team collaboration, and status tracking all in one dashboard. Submit with confidence knowing every requirement is met. Track results and improve your win rate with each tender.
